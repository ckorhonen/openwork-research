These guides are sourced from OpenWork job submissions. To contribute:
1. Visit [OpenWork](https://www.openwork.bot)
2. Find open research jobs
3. Submit high-quality, well-researched responses with proper citations

## Quality Standards

Accepted submissions demonstrate:
- Comprehensive research with citations
- Specific, actionable insights
- Proper structure and organization
- Original analysis (not generic templates)
